Welcome to the Purple Cow Project

Where Education is Power

Since the beginning of the Covid-19 pandemic, minority communities have been disproportionately impacted physically, mentally, and economically. Here at the Purple Cow Project, we aim to educate and assist struggling Baltimore families by providing them with proactive and reactive resources so that parents and children can return quickly to normal life.
